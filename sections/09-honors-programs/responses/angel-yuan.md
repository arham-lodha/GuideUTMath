# Angel on Dean's Scholars as a math major

*Written April 2026, graduated 2026, now at UCLA.*

## Why did you join Dean's Scholars, and in hindsight was it worth it? (~200 words)

Dean's Scholars advertises itself as a program for people who are interested in research, and I want to go into math research, but as it turns out, math undergraduates don't really get to do much research, at least not at a significant level. I don't think I really gained nor lost anything major from being in the program. 

## What did your thesis actually look like, and how did you land on that format? (~250 words)

It's an expository paper on stable homotopy theory. I was advised by Irving Dai, who doesn't do stable homotopy theory, so I mostly was just self-studying and writing things up with weekly check-ins. 

## What did you do about the bio/chem-coded programming? (~150 words)

I took the honours genetics sequence my freshman year, and rhetoric and waves my sophomore fall, so I got the extra requirements out of the way immediately and never had to worry about them after that.

## How did Dean's Scholars interact with your grad school apps? (~150 words)

I don't really think it interacted with my grad school apps directly. Being in Dean's meant I got to talk to Dr. Starbird as a freshman, and he wrote a strong letter for me when I was applying to REUs that year, and my advisor from my freshman REU specifically cited this letter as a reason for inviting me. That advisor was very impressed by my mathematical ability and wrote a letter for my junior year REU, and I used a letter from that REU in grad admissions, so there's a bit of a trickle-down effect. 

## What would you tell a math freshman weighing whether to apply / accept? (~200 words)

It doesn't really hurt to do either and you can always drop it later on if you don't feel like doing the breadth requirement. You get to dodge MPA advising (they suck) and interact with Maggie more, so those are nice. I didn't have to take computational or intro proof classes with the way the honours degree is set up.
