# Research and REUs

**Format:** structured responses (individual voice, same questions) + a synthesized overview of the REU landscape.

## What goes here

- The REU landscape: which programs UT math students have attended, how to think about selection, timeline, application mechanics
- Research at UT with faculty (reading projects, summer research, continuing work into the year)
- Cross-disciplinary and cross-institution research (physics REUs, MIT labs, etc.)
- What "research" actually means in math at the undergraduate level — and what it often isn't

## Format

The REU landscape overview is synthesized by the steward. The trajectory-and-experience content is structured responses: each contributor answers the same questions in their own voice, and responses are presented side-by-side so readers can compare.
