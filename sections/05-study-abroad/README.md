# Study abroad

**Format:** individual essays, one per program.

## What goes here

Narrative accounts of study-abroad experiences relevant to math majors — what the program was like, how it fit into the timeline, what it cost in other opportunities, whether you'd recommend it and to whom.

Known programs in our cohort:
- **ETH Zürich** — Arham
- [add as contributors come in]

## Format

Each program gets one essay per contributor who went. Write in your own voice, narrative form, 800–1500 words.

## Suggested structure (use it or don't)

- Why you picked this program
- The application and logistics (money, credit transfer, timing in your degree)
- The math: courses, professors, what the level and style were like relative to UT
- The non-math: living abroad, social life, what you did with your time off
- Effect on grad school apps, letters, research trajectory
- Who this program is a good fit for — and who it isn't
- What you'd do differently
